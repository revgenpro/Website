Medical Billing

Our comprehensive medical billing services are designed to maximize your reimbursements while reducing your administrative burden. We handle the entire billing process, from claim submission to payment posting, allowing your staff to focus on patient care.

Our Medical Billing Services Include:

  • Accurate and timely claim submission
  • Charge entry and coding review
  • Electronic and paper claims processing
  • Payment posting and reconciliation
  • Patient statement generation
  • Detailed financial reporting

Claims Management

Our claims management services ensure that your claims are processed correctly the first time, reducing denials and accelerating reimbursements. We use advanced technology and industry expertise to optimize every step of the claims process.

Our Claims Management Services Include:

  • Pre-submission claim scrubbing and validation
  • Electronic claim submission to all payers
  • Claim tracking and status monitoring
  • Rejection analysis and correction
  • Appeals management
  • Performance reporting and analytics

Eligibility Verification

Our eligibility verification services help you confirm patient insurance coverage and benefits before providing services, reducing claim denials and improving your cash flow. We verify coverage for all appointments, ensuring you have the information you need to make informed financial decisions.

Our Eligibility Verification Services Include:

  • Real-time insurance verification
  • Benefit coverage confirmation
  • Co-pay, deductible, and co-insurance verification
  • Prior authorization requirements
  • Patient responsibility estimation
  • Verification reporting

Revenue Analytics

Our revenue analytics services provide you with valuable insights into your financial performance, helping you identify opportunities for improvement and make data-driven decisions. We deliver comprehensive reports and dashboards that give you a clear picture of your revenue cycle.

Our Revenue Analytics Services Include:

  • Custom financial dashboards
  • Key performance indicator tracking
  • Payer performance analysis
  • Provider productivity reporting
  • Trend analysis and forecasting
  • Benchmarking against industry standards

Denial Management

Our denial management services help you identify, address, and prevent claim denials, improving your reimbursement rates and reducing revenue leakage. We take a proactive approach to denial management, addressing the root causes of denials to prevent them from recurring.

Our Denial Management Services Include:

  • Denial analysis and root cause identification
  • Denial prevention strategies
  • Appeals management
  • Payer-specific denial trends
  • Staff education and training
  • Denial reporting and analytics

Accounts Receivable

Our accounts receivable services help you optimize your cash flow by effectively managing outstanding balances and following up on unpaid claims. We work diligently to collect what you're owed, reducing your days in A/R and improving your bottom line.

Our Accounts Receivable Services Include:

  • A/R aging analysis
  • Payer follow-up and resolution
  • Patient balance management
  • Payment plan administration
  • Bad debt reduction strategies
  • A/R performance reporting
