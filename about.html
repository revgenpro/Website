Our Story

How RevGenPro RCM became a leader in healthcare revenue cycle management

Founded in 2010, RevGenPro RCM was established with a clear mission: to help healthcare providers navigate the increasingly complex world of medical billing and revenue cycle management.

Our founders, with over 30 years of combined experience in healthcare finance and technology, recognized that many providers were struggling with declining reimbursements, increasing regulations, and administrative burdens that took time away from patient care.

Starting with just a handful of clients, we've grown to serve hundreds of healthcare organizations across the country, from small independent practices to large multi-specialty groups and hospitals.

Throughout our growth, we've remained committed to our core values of integrity, innovation, and exceptional service. We measure our success not just by our clients' improved financial performance, but by the lasting partnerships we build.
