Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you implement your RCM services for our practice?

Our implementation timeline typically ranges from 2-4 weeks, depending on the size and complexity of your practice. We have a structured onboarding process that ensures a smooth transition with minimal disruption to your operations.

What makes RevGenPro RCM different from other RCM service providers?

We combine advanced technology with specialized healthcare expertise to deliver tailored solutions for each client. Our approach focuses on not just managing your revenue cycle, but optimizing it for maximum financial performance. We also provide dedicated account managers and transparent reporting.

How do you charge for your services?

We offer flexible pricing models, including percentage-based fees, flat monthly rates, or hybrid models. The specific structure depends on your practice size, specialty, and the services you need. We'll work with you to find the most cost-effective solution for your organization.

Can you work with our existing EHR/PM system?

Yes, we integrate with all major EHR and practice management systems. Our team has experience with over 50 different healthcare software platforms, and we can seamlessly connect with your existing systems without requiring you to change your current setup.
